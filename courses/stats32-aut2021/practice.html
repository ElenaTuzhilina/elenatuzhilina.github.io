Shortcuts:

  • Cmd-Enter in Mac or Ctrl-Enter in Windows/Linux executes the highlighted text. If no text is highlighted, execute the current line of code the cursor is on.

  • Option-Cmd-I in Mac or Ctrl-Alt-I in Windows/Linux inserts a new code chunk.

  • Cmd-Shift-K in Mac or Ctrl-Shift-K in Windows/Linux knits the document.

To learn more, open RStudio and click Option-Shift-K in Mac or Alt-Shift-K in Windows/Linux.
